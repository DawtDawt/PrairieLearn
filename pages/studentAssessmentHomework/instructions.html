Instructions from HTML!:

  • Give each team member at least one role
  • Each role (except contributor) must have at least one student assigned
  • If you have more team members than roles, extra team members can be given the contributor role

Manager
Required: 1 Manager
  • Makes sure group starts quickly and remains focused during the activity.
  • Takes care of time management.
  • Makes sure all voices in the group are heard.
Recorder
Required: 1 Recorder
  • Records important aspects of group discussions, observations, and insights.
  • Records names and roles of group members if necessary.
  • Transcribes and submits the group's answers after discussing the problems.
Reflector
Required: 1 Reflector
  • Guides consensus-building process; group must agree on responses to questions.
  • Reports to the group periodically during the activity on how the group performs.
  • Submits any reflection-related surveys at the end of the activity.
Contributor
Required: 0 Contributors
  • Fills in the gaps not covered by other group members.
  • Supports the group through looking up documentation, researching answers, etc.
  • Communicates questions, clarifications, and insights to the group.
